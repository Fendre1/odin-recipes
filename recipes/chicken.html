Baked Chicken in BBQ Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1/2 a cup of BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 a cup of soy sauce
  • 1/4 a cup of red wine
  • 1/4 a cup of maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon of chilli powder
  • 1 tablespoon of dry mustard powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 4 skinless boneless chicken halves
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • 1 cup of water

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees C
  2. Whisk together BBQ sauce, soy sauce, red wine, maple syrup, chilli powder, mustard powder, onion powder and cumin until thoroughly combined.
  3. Place chicken breasts into a 9x13 inch pan. Pour sauce over the chicken and turn the chicken pieces over in the sauce to cover both sides
  4. Bake the chicken in the preheated oven for 40 minutes. Use a thermometer to check if the inside of the chicken has reached 70 degrees C.
  5. Pour remainder of sauce mixture into a saucepan, place over medium heat, bring to a boil. Whisk cornstarch in a bowl until smooth, mix cornstarch into sauce mixture and reduce heat to a simmer. Allow the sauce to thicken, whisk constantly. Pour sauce over chicken to serve.
