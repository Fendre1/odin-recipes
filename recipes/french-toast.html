Lemon-Vanilla French Toast

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup of half-and-half
  • 5 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons of confectioners' sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon of lemon extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 6 slices of bread
  • 1/2 cup of maple syrup
  • 3 tablespoons of whipped cream

Instructions

  1. Combine half-and-half, egg whites, confectioners' sugar, brown sugar, vanilla extract, lemon extract and salt in a bowl. Whisk mixture until well blended. Pour mixture into a flat container
  2. Put a bread slice into the container and flip it so that it would be covered on both sides
  3. Add 1 tablespoon of butter to a frying pan over medium heat. Add the soaked bread slice to the pan and fry for 1-2 minutes or until both sides are golden brown and crispy. Continue cooking bread slices in batches, first dipping into the prepared mixture and then frying. Add more butter to frying pan if needed.
  4. Serve French toast with maple syrup and whipped cream on top.
