Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces of lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound of lean ground beef
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1/2 a pound of grated mozarella cheese
  • 1/2 a pound of grated cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint of ricotta cheese

Instructions

  1. Bring large pot of lightly salted water to boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al denta. Drain the pasta.
  2. Preheat oven to 175 degrees C. In a large skillet over medium heat, brown beef and season with salt and pepper; drain. Add spaghetti sauce and minced garlic; allow to simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. In a medium bowl add mozarella, cheddar and ricotta; stir well. In a 9x13 inch pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture and cheese mixture until pan is filled. Top with cheese mixture.
  4. Add pan to oven and bake for 30 minutes or until cheese has begun to brown.
